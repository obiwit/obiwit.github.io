Introduction

By Beatriz Borges R, edited on Mar 16, 12018

I couldn’t find a book on machine learning that told me what I wanted to know, so I decided to compile my own notes.

The idea is that these notes will improve as I know more of Machine Learning – as I deepen my understanding of several concepts, and subsequently explain these concepts better. Right now, my notes are at a more high-level point of view, without much explanation of the more nitty-gritty details - though I already have some plans to change that in a few months, after I've finished laying down the first foundation!

I’m sharing them in the hope they can be useful to somebody else! Feedback is always appreciated, so feel free to drop me a note if I made a mistake ;)

When my notes are more substantial, I will also add a pdf of the most recent version of them here.
